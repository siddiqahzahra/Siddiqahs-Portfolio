Genesis BATTLE OF CHAMPIONS
Web - iOS

Project Overview

The goal of this project was to design a website for users to gather details regarding the Genesis Battle of Champions game. Users are able to view the rulebooks, read about the champions, and learn how to host the game.

My Contributions

As the only designer on the project, I created all the designs myself in Figma. From there, I moved the designs over to Webflow and ensured the designs fit all screens from Web to mobile. I was involved in everything from defining the brand and personas, creating flows and wireframes, all the way to creating final UI designs.
